Welcome to LuMo

LuMo is a web and mobile based platform that is designed to help small and medium enterprices to help monitor their stores. The app offers features such as keeping track of inventory, movement of products, keeping a detailed list of orders, list of all products paid for and any that are in credit. It also provides the business owner a way to track the progress of the business and monitors when they made profits and losses.

Keep a list all products that you sell.

Find a well kept summary of everything you have sold and ordered daily, weekly and monthly. You can also find a summary of all incomplete purchases.

Create a list of each and every order that also contributes to keeping track of your inventory to avoid running low on some products. You can as well as separate paid and incomplete orders allowing you to keep track of any credit owed to the store.

Keep track of all your inventory. Know when and which products are required to be added to the store. Avoid spending your money on stock you already have and running out of stock.
